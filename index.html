Data intrigues me. They go on PowerPoints. I love how people use data and try to learn from it. Since I was fortunate, or you could say unfortunate, helping an extended family member with her business, I saw the operational side of a business that I didn’t get to see when I was working as a salesperson for a retail chain. From that experience I can sort-of think like a boss, and I am assuming for prospective employers that they have the same questions I have — what is going on under the hood? From the Data Analyst Bootcamp I am currently attending, I have learned a lot, but I feel I have barely scratched the surface. I’m excited to keep learning and growing in my current student role.
